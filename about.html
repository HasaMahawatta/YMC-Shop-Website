About Our Company

YCM Computer Shop was founded in 2014 and has quickly grown to become one of the Kegalle's leading retailers of latest technology products. We are located in a large head office in Essex where we can operate quickly and efficiently in order to serve the demands of our ever growing customer base! Our goal is simple, "To bring our customers exceptional technology from the leading brands at the best prices." We are passionate about providing you with the best shopping experience from your very first stage, to the very last. Your shopping journey is our number one priority! Our fully trained staff are always up-to-date with the very latest technologies even before the products hit the shelves. This is why our customers keep coming back to us! .
